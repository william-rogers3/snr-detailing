About

Car Detailing is an Art Form.


S&R Detailing was founded by Matthew Shinn and William Rogers in 2021. Our team is dedicated to ensuring that your car looks and feels like new again. Whether you need a basic wash or a complete interior and exterior detailing, S&R Detailing has the expertise and equipment to get the job done right. We use only the highest-quality products and techniques to clean and restore your vehicle to its original condition.


At S&R Detailing, customer satisfaction is our top priority. We strive to deliver exceptional service and exceed our clients' expectations on every job. If you're looking for a reliable and professional auto detailing service in Cleveland, look no further than S&R Detailing.
